Earth's Diverse Ecosystems

Exploring the planet's natural habitats

Rainforests

Rainforests are home to more than half of the world's plant and animal species. They play a crucial role in regulating Earth's climate.

Deserts

Despite harsh conditions, deserts support unique life forms adapted to extreme temperatures and limited water.

Oceans

Oceans cover 71% of Earth's surface and contain 97% of the planet's water, supporting incredible biodiversity.
